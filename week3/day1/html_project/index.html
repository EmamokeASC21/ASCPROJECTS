MY FAVORITE RAPPER: POLO G

Where it all Started

Polo G's real name is Taurus Tremani Bartlett and he was born Born in 1999 and raised in the rough neighborhood of northern Chicago, Polo G was rapping by the time he was a teenager. His earliest songs appeared in 2017, but it wasn't until his breakout single "Finer Things" that the rapper began to really take off. The motivational song was written when Polo was incarcerated, and once he was free, he recorded it and made a video to go with it, which immediately caught the attention of millions.
